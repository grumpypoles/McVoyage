Kawakawa Station

Getting There

Kawakawa Station is located in Southern Wairarapa. It takes only a couple of hours to get there from our place. We were expected to arrive at the station in the late afternoon so we decided to stop for lunch and window shopping in Martinborough. The Village Cafe and Bar was a good choice. I can not remember what we ate but I can recollect strong, hot drinks. The rest of the afternoon we spent visiting multiple small stores selling a variety of items, some of which I had no idea what are they for. The last visited shop was a Four Square food market where we stocked up on wine and beer before continuing our journey for another hour. Upon arriving at the station we were welcome by the owners and shown to our accommodation. That evening we met other members of the Scenic Walkers group, had a nice dinner prepared for us by the host and were introduced to the logistic of the walk.

Day 01

Farm Cottage to Jake's Cabins

23-Oct-2020

It was a very interesting day. It started innocently with an easy walk along the beach towards Ngawi settlement. This coast is well known to Wellington water enthusiasts so it was not a big surprise to see Magnus there having a go on the SUP. Once we reached the Ngawi golf club we turned left towards the hills. At the bottom of the hill we had a short rest around the public toilet and then started a slow ascend to the top of 'The Ant' at 616 meters above sea level. Overall the climb was not too difficult but had its moment. Once we reach the summit we were awarded spectacular 360-degree views. From there the track leads all way down to Jake's Cabins. All our gear, food and drinks were already delivered by the host. All we needed to do was to heat the semi cooked meals and enjoy dinner in a big common room.
